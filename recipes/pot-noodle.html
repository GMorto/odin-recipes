Pot Noodle Recipe

Description

It is noodles... in a pot

Ingretients

  • the pot noodle itself
  • boiling water
  • extra soy sauce

Instructions

  1. open the pot noodle lid leaving it in tack on one side (we will explain why later)
  2. pour boiling water into the pot up to the designated line
  3. close the lid over the pot and leave to stand for 3-4 minutes. Closing the lid allows the steam to heat it through more effectively in additio to the boilding water
  4. pour the soy sauce in and enjoy!
